📸 How to Add Property Photos

1
Go to Imgur
https://imgur.com/upload Opens in new tab - no account needed
2
Upload your photos
Drag & drop or click to select your property images
✅ Supported: JPG, PNG, GIF (max 20MB per image)
3
Copy the image link
After upload, you'll see your image. On the right side, find and click "Copy Link"
4
Paste the link below
Come back here and paste the link in the "Image URL" field
💡 Pro Tips:
  • Upload 5-10 high-quality photos
  • First image will be the cover photo
  • Include: bedroom, bathroom, living area, kitchen, exterior, view
  • Make sure photos are well-lit and clean
